# Monopoluri

## Description
A private web application for a married couple to track and manage their shared and individual finances with complete transparency. Features AI-powered insights, goal tracking, and a playful, game-like interface that makes financial responsibility feel collaborative and rewarding.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Not knowing where money goes
Track every transaction with clear categories, owner assignment (Eden/Sivan/Shared), and visual differentiation by profile color to see exactly where every dollar is spent.

### Problem 2: High cost of premium finance apps
Build your own custom solution and save on monthly subscription fees while getting exactly the features you need.

### Problem 3: Lack of actionable insights
AI-powered analysis that suggests optimizations and answers questions about spending patterns, helping you make smarter financial decisions.

### Problem 4: Financial management feels like a chore
Gamified experience with shared goals, progress bars, and celebration animations that make managing money together feel rewarding and fun.

## Key Features
- Profile selection with unique color identities for Eden and Sivan
- Comprehensive transaction tracking (income/expenses, categories, frequency, photo receipts)
- Shared and individual financial goals with visual progress tracking
- Analytics dashboard with spending breakdowns and trend visualization
- AI insights page with automated suggestions and conversational Q&A

## Product Roadmap

### 1. Transactions
Track all income and expenses with categories, frequencies, and owner assignment.

### 2. Goals
Create and manage shared or personal financial goals with progress tracking.

### 3. Analytics
Visualize spending patterns, trends, and financial behavior through charts and graphs.

### 4. AI Insights
Get automated suggestions and ask conversational questions about your finances.
